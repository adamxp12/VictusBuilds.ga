About Me

I am Victus, otherwise known as iVictus in the Minecraft and planet minecraft communitys. I've been building in the game for just over a year and a half now and I've been passionate about minecraft ever since i got the game in late 2011.

Over my time playing the game i have watched some of the most highly established groups of people push the game to its limits creating outstanding structures and natural organics using just a few blocks and a bit of thought.

Although I have watched these people and groups I have also learnt from them, gained other skills and advanced myself to suit the building communtiy of the game.
