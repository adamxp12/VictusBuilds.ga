About Us

Victus is a new fresh build team innovating and improving builds of many different styles. We work with clients and servers to make sure they get the best quality build and that there payment is safe. Our website shows most of our projects some sneak peaks at what we are working on and disclaimers behind builds and builders within our community.

We try to make our builds good quality for time-lapses cinematics and purposeful builds. We work with many different people creating adventure maps, spawns PVP maps plots and many other things for people in different areas of the Minecraft community.

We will be uploading to our YouTube channel however our graphics and animations still needs roughing up before we edit and upload our amazing cinematics and builds.

Some of our builds will be downloadable to the public however private client builds will only be available in video format or on servers as the iconic spawns or amazing backdrops.
