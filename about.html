About Victus Build Team

About Us

Victus Builds is a minecraft build team that innovate and produce amazing minecraft maps, not only can you watch our amazing cinematics. You can also help build by becoming a member of the Arkena minecraft server and ranking your way up to Expert where you will then be assessed if you are worthy for the Build Team (BT.) you will be assessed by the servers strongest builders and also be questioned and researched to see if your builds are truly your own inspiration.

VictusBuilds is mainly a build team however we are going to be organising branch YouTube channels where we will be posting games; minecraft lets plays and graphics hosted by DevilsHead.

You can also order specialised builds with the imputs of your dimensions e.g. 200x200 block build would be about $175 - $200 (fluctuating depending on the category you pick) category consist of,
rush job(standard price),
steady(+15usd)
or rapid(+35usd), (the faster the category the more you would pay)

Victus will also be hosting community competitions e.g. design a, hub, portal, town, air craft, terrain and much more. For you guys to get involved in. The judging period of these competitions will be long as you are assessed on originality, idea, atmosphere, style and detail / depth. The styles being most important as you need it to be the same style all the way throughout a build.

Victus will be having a launch date however it is not specific yet, once we have a specific launch we may consider a count down on the server if possible.
